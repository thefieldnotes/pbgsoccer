How Top Tier Is Assigned
Assignment is dynamic, not fixed to a league name. Top Tier is computed per birth year, per season — whichever tier a cohort actually fielded that season with the highest global rank becomes their Top Tier. A cohort that fielded ECNL in prior seasons but not in 2025/26 will have their next-highest tier become Top Tier automatically.
Rank reflects competitive level, not in-season performance. A non-top-tier team posting a high win rate does not elevate it above a top-tier team on the competitive ordinal — rank is structural, not performance-based.
